Plugin Features

Multi-tab Action

ROBORELAY works on multiple windows and tabs. The tool customizes your organization's needs on every tab. Complete control over auto booking from the speed of refresh rate to the ideal routes for your journeys.

Rate-Refresher

Set the refresh rate to milliseconds (ms) increments. Randomize adds an extra chance to book and extra security by randomizing Refresh Rate Frequency to mimic a human dispatcher to access "hot loads."

Hand-Pick Payouts

Set the minimum payout, and the ROBORELAY rate will be the highest available payout. You can use one or both of these features together to book the most optimal payout rates.

Prime time selection

Set the time range feature to book the ideal time for the first pick up of your driver. This is a built-in ROBORELAY clock that starts when you click Start and books only in the time window you set.

Custom build routes

Exclude States from the route to make your journeys more productive and safer. You can exclude as many States as you like.
